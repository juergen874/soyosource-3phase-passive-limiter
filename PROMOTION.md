# Why this Mod?

The **Soyosource 3-Phase Passive Limiter** is a cost-effective, simple, and robust way to bring 3-phase monitoring to your single-phase inverter.

### Key Benefits:
- **Zero Software Needed:** No ESP32 or programming required. 100% passive components.
- **Reliable:** High-quality resistor network ensures stability.
- **Easy Calibration:** Simply loop the wires through the sensor—no complex tuning.
- **Open Source:** Based on real-world measurements and forum-driven engineering.

Upgrade your Soyosource setup today and achieve smarter, multi-phase zero-export control!
